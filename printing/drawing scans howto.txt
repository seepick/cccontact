TIPS making pictures:
*) camera is straight (no perspective distortion)
*) lamp is _below_ camera (but no shadow cast)
*) lamp is also straight
*) lots of light, but not too much/bright


GIMP:
*) Colors / Saturation ... totally down (no color)
*) Colors / Brigntess-Contrast ... both 30-50
*) Colors / Curves ... like an S-shape
*) cut out, feather 150


or:
*) Colors / Hue-Chroma ... chroma MIN, lightness bit up
*) Colors / Exposure ... nah
*) Colors /  componentent
*) Colors /  kanalenmixer ... blue down, https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9HR3IgftbDw
